# FraudShield AI System

FraudShield is an AI-assisted fraud decisioning platform for banking transactions.
It evaluates card and account transactions using machine learning models, rule-based checks, device intelligence, and analyst review workflows.

The system uses risk scores from models such as XGBoost and Vowpal Wabbit, combines them with deterministic business rules, and returns a decision such as approve, review, or block.

High-risk transactions are routed to human analysts with explanations, evidence, and recommended next actions.
